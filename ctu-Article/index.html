WELCOME TO CTU- DANAO CAMPUS

An article highlighting stories and photos around the campus.

History

Cebu Technological University (CTU) Danao was established in June 1949, following a resolution by the Danao Municipal Council, initially named "Danao Provincial High School." On June 22, 1957, it was transformed into a national vocational secondary school under Republic Act (R.A.) No. 1907. In 1958, it was renamed Danao National Vocational School and placed under the supervision of the Cebu School of Arts and Trades in Cebu City.

In 1963, the school secured an eight-hectare site in Barangay Sabang, and by 1964, it had two buildings with shop machines donated by the Colombo Aid from New Zealand. In July 1965, the school moved to its current location in Sabang, Danao City.

Ten years later, it became Danao Vocational School and began offering a two-year trade technical program. In 1983, it became part of the Cebu State College of Science and Technology (CSCST) under Batas Pambansa 412. The following year, it became an independent chartered college called CSCST-College of Industrial Technology, led by a Vocational School Superintendent. By 1988, an administration building and a two-story building were constructed.

In 1990, the school phased out its secondary education curriculum to concentrate on higher education programs, eventually graduating its first batch of baccalaureate degree students. Through the efforts of Dr. Bonifacio Villanueva and the backing of several legislators, President Arroyo signed a bill in 2009 that transformed the Cebu State College of Science and Technology System, along with its external campuses, into Cebu Technological University.

Today, CTU Danao is one of the top campuses of the university, offering 19 undergraduate and 9 graduate programs to more than 4,156 students. Many of these programs have been accredited by the Accrediting Agency of Chartered Colleges and Universities in the Philippines (AACCUP) with Level II and III accreditations.

Gallery

Here are some photos taken inside the campus, showcasing the vibrant academic environment at Cebu Technological University (CTU) - Danao Campus.

Each photo reflects the spirit of collaboration, creativity, and engagement that defines the CTU - Danao Campus.

Entrance

The entrance of CTU Danao Campus welcomes students and visitors with a modern and inviting design. There are decorative elements and clear signage to guide newcomers, while nearby seating invites moments of relaxation. This area serves as a lively gateway and vibrant academic environment filled with opportunities for growth and connection.

School Building

The school building at CTU Danao is a modern facility that enhances the learning experience. It features spacious classrooms equipped with contemporary teaching tools and technology. The school building features an open layout that encourages interaction among students and enriching the educational experience.

Library

The library at CTU Danao is an essential resource hub for students, providing a space to study and relax featuring a vast collection of books, journals, and digital materials that cater to diverse academic fields. It is quiet and welcoming, with comfortable study areas and modern seating that promote focus and collaboration.

Bistro Canteen

The CTU Danao cafeteria is a lively dining space where students and staff can enjoy a variety of meals and snacks. It offers a welcoming atmosphere with plenty of seating, making it a perfect place to take a break and interact with.

Study Area

The study area at CTU Danao is a area where students can unwind, focus on their studies, or socialize with other people. Students also can access the complimentary internet, enhancing their experience while taking a break from classes.

Hallway

The hallways at CTU Danao Campus are open and well-lit for the students and teachers to walk around searching for their classroom, library, etc. The spacious corridors allow students to gather and interact, making it a lively hub of campus life.

Kadasig Gym

The Kadasig Gym at CTU Danao is a spacious facility designed for sports, physical education, and various recreational activities. It provides a space for team sports and fitness classes. Also, it hosts various events, including competitions and school gatherings.

Oval/Grandstand

The Oval also known as the Grandstand, is a prominent feature of CTU Danao Campus, designed for various athletic events and school gatherings. This expansive area features a wide track and field, providing plenty of space for sports and recreational activities.
